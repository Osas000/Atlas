# Purpose

Mission Control is the heart of Atlas.

It is not merely a dashboard.

It is the operational headquarters from which every workday begins and ends.

Every recommendation, opportunity, workflow, reminder, and performance metric is coordinated through Mission Control.

The objective is simple:

**Always tell the user what the highest-value action is right now.**

---

# Core Philosophy

Mission Control answers six questions immediately.

1. What should I do now?

2. Why should I do it?

3. How much can I earn?

4. How long will it take?

5. What is blocking me?

6. What should I do after this?

If the interface cannot answer these six questions within a few seconds, it has failed.

---

# Design Principles

Mission Control is designed for action.

Never decoration.

Never unnecessary widgets.

Never information overload.

Everything displayed must contribute to productive work.
